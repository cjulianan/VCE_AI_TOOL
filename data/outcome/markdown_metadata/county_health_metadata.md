# 2025 County Health Rankings Virginia Data - v4.csv

**Description:** This dataset contains county-level health rankings and related data for the state of Virginia, covering various health indicators and social determinants of health. The data is sourced from the County Health Rankings & Roadmaps program and provides insights into the health status and factors affecting health outcomes in Virginia counties.

- **Source Organization:** County Health Rankings & Roadmaps
- **Geographic Coverage:** county
- **Temporal Coverage:** 2022-2024
- **URL Source:** [Link](https://www.countyhealthrankings.org/health-data/virginia/data-and-resources)
- **Spatial Alignment:** FIPS Column: `FIPS`, Locality Column: `County`

### Column Schema

* **`Average Number of Physically Unhealthy Days`** (*float*): Average number of reported physically unhealthy days per month *(Keywords: poor health, poor physical, bad health, bad health days)*
* **`% Low Birth Weight`** (*float*): Percentage of live births with low birth weight *(Keywords: low birth weight, low birth, birth weight)*
* **`Average Number of Mentally Unhealthy Days`** (*float*): Average number of reported mentally unhealthy days per month *(Keywords: poor mental health, poor mental, bad mental health, bad mental health days)*
* **`% Fair or Poor Health`** (*float*): Percentage of adults reporting fair or poor health *(Keywords: fair health, poor health, bad health)*
* **`% Vaccinated`** (*float*): Percentage of adults who have received recommended vaccines *(Keywords: vaccinated, vaccine, immunized)*
* **`% Smokers`** (*float*): Percentage of adults who are current smokers *(Keywords: smokers, smoking, tobacco)*
* **`% With Access to Exercise Opportunities`** (*float*): Percentage of adults with access to exercise opportunities *(Keywords: exercise, physical activity, fitness, access to exercise)*
* **`Food Environment Index`** (*float*): Index measuring the availability and quality of healthy food options in the county, on a scale of 1-10, with higher scores indicating better food environments. *(Keywords: food environment, food access, nutrition, food quality, healthy food)*
* **`# Primary Care Physicians`** (*int*): Number of primary care physician in each county *(Keywords: primary care physicians, doctors, healthcare providers)*
* **`Primary Care Physicians Rate`** (*float*): Rate of primary care physicians per 100,000 people *(Keywords: primary care physicians rate, doctors rate, healthcare providers rate, rate of primary care physicians)*
* **`# Mental Health Providers`** (*int*): Number of mental health providers in each county *(Keywords: mental health providers, therapists, counselors, psychologists)*
* **`Mental Health Provider Rate`** (*float*): Rate of mental health providers per 100,000 people in each county *(Keywords: mental health provider rate, therapists rate, counselors rate, psychologists rate, rate of mental health providers)*
* **`# Dentists`** (*int*): Number of dentists in each county *(Keywords: dentists, dental providers, oral health providers)*
* **`Dentist Rate`** (*float*): Rate of dentists per 100,000 people in each county *(Keywords: dentist rate, dental providers rate, oral health providers rate, rate of dentists)*
* **`Preventable Hospitalization Rate`** (*float*): Discharges for Ambulatory Care Sensitive Conditions per 100,000 Medicare Enrollees *(Keywords: preventable hospitalization rate, hospitalization rate, preventable hospitalizations)*
* **`% with Annual Mammogram`** (*float*): Percentage of female Medicare enrollees having an annual mammogram (age 65-74) *(Keywords: annual mammogram, mammogram, breast cancer screening, breast cancer detection)*
* **`% Uninsured`** (*float*): Percentage of the county under age 65 that is uninsured *(Keywords: uninsured, health insurance, coverage, no insurance)*
* **`% Severe Housing Problems`** (*float*): Percentage of households with at least 1 of 4 housing problems: overcrowding, high housing costs, or lack of kitchen or plumbing facilities *(Keywords: severe housing problems, housing issues, housing problems, housing challenges)*
* **`% Long Commute - Drives Alone`** (*float*): Among workers who commute in their car alone, the percentage that commute more than 30 minutes *(Keywords: long commute, drives alone, commuting alone, single driver commute)*
* **`Average Daily PM2.5`** (*float*): Average daily amount of fine particulate matter in micrograms per cubic meter (PM2.5) in the air. An indication of air quality. *(Keywords: pm2.5, air pollution, particulate matter, air quality)*
* **`Presence of Water Violation`** (*string*): Indicates with a Yes/No whether there has been a water violation in the county *(Keywords: water violation, water quality, water contamination, water safety)*
* **`% Households with Broadband Access`** (*float*): Percentage of households in the county that have broadband access *(Keywords: broadband access, internet access, high speed internet, digital connectivity)*
* **`% Completed High School`** (*float*): Percentage of adults age 25 and over with a high school diploma or equivalent *(Keywords: completed high school, high school graduation, high school graduates, education level, high school completion)*
* **`% Children in Poverty`** (*float*): Percentage of children under age 18 living in poverty *(Keywords: children in poverty, child poverty, kids in poverty, youth poverty, poor kids, poor children)*
* **`# Injury Deaths`** (*int*): Number of injury-related deaths in the county *(Keywords: injury deaths, accidental deaths, fatal injuries, trauma deaths)*
* **`Injury Death Rate`** (*float*): Rate of injury-related deaths per 100,000 population in the county *(Keywords: injury death rate, accidental death rate, fatal injury rate, trauma death rate, rate of injury deaths)*
* **`% Household Income Required for Child Care Expenses`** (*float*): Percentage of median household income required for child care expenses for two children *(Keywords: child care expenses, child care costs, child care affordability, child care income percentage)*
