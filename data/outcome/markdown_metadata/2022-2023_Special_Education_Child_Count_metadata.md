# 2022-2023_Special_Education_Child_Count.csv

**Description:** Count of special education needs students by and school

- **Source Organization:** Virginia Department of Education
- **Geographic Coverage:** county
- **Temporal Coverage:** 2022-2023
- **URL Source:** [Link](https://data.virginia.gov/dataset/special-education-child-count-2022-2023)
- **Spatial Alignment:** FIPS Column: `fips_code`, Locality Column: `Division Name`

### Column Schema

* **`School Year`** (*int*): The academic school year the data was reported in. *(Keywords: school year, academic calander, year, time)*
* **`Division Name`** (*string*): The school district/county in Virginia where the data was reported in *(Keywords: division name, county, county name, location, place)*
* **`fips_code`** (*int*): Federal Information Processing Series unique numerical code identifier for the county. *(Keywords: fips code, county code)*
* **`School Number`** (*int*): The specific school number identifier for particular school in dataset. *(Keywords: school number, school identifier)*
* **`School Name`** (*string*): The specific school name for particular school in dataset. *(Keywords: school name, school)*
* **`Total Count`** (*int*): Total number of students needing special education in particular school. *(Keywords: total count, special education count, count, number)*
