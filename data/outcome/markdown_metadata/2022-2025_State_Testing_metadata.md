# 2022-2025_State_Testing.csv

**Description:** Pass rates of state testing by subject by school

- **Source Organization:** Virginia Department of Education
- **Geographic Coverage:** county
- **Temporal Coverage:** 2022-2025
- **URL Source:** [Link](https://www.doe.virginia.gov/data-policy-funding/data-reports/statistics-reports/sol-test-pass-rates-other-results)
- **Spatial Alignment:** FIPS Column: `fips_code`, Locality Column: `Div Name`

### Column Schema

* **`Div Name`** (*string*): The name of the school division or school district in Virginia. *(Keywords: division name, county, county name, location, place)*
* **`fips_code`** (*int*): Federal Information Processing Series unique numerical code identifier for the county. *(Keywords: fips code, county code)*
* **`Sch Name`** (*string*): The specific school name for particular school in dataset. *(Keywords: school name, school)*
* **`Sch Type`** (*int*): The specific school name for particular school in dataset (e.g. elementary, middle school, high school). *(Keywords: school type, school classification)*
* **`Low Grade`** (*string*): The lowest grade level offered or tested at this specific school. *(Keywords: lowest grade, starting grade)*
* **`High Grade`** (*string*): The highest grade level offered or tested at this specific school. *(Keywords: highest grade, ending grade)*
* **`Subject`** (*string*): The academic subject area of the Standards of Learning (SOL) test. *(Keywords: subject, test subject, Standards of Learning, SOL, math, english, reading, science, history, social sciences)*
* **`Student Group`** (*string*): The demographic or performance subgroup being reported. *(Keywords: student group, demographic, ethnicity, race, gender)*
* **`2022-2023 Numerator`** (*int*): The number of students who passed the SOL test during the 2022-2023 school year. *(Keywords: 2022-2023 numerator, 2022 passed, 2023 passed)*
* **`2022-2023 Denominator`** (*int*): The total number of students who took the SOL test during the 2022-2023 school year. *(Keywords: 2022-2023 denominator, 2022 total tested, 2022 total tested)*
* **`2022-2023 Pass Rate`** (*int*): The percentage of students who passed the SOL test in 2022-2023. *(Keywords: 2022-2023 pass rate, 2022 pass-rate, 2022 pass-rate)*
* **`2023-2024 Numerator`** (*int*): The number of students who passed the SOL test during the 2023-2024 school year. *(Keywords: 2023-2024 numerator, 2023 passed, 2024 passed)*
* **`2023-2024 Denominator`** (*int*): The total number of students who took the SOL test during the 2023-2024 school year. *(Keywords: 2022-2023 denominator, 2023 total tested, 2024 total tested)*
* **`2023-2024 Pass Rate`** (*int*): The percentage of students who passed the SOL test in 2023-2024. *(Keywords: 2023-2024 pass rate, 2023 pass-rate, 2024 pass-rate)*
* **`2024-2025 Numerator`** (*int*): The number of students who passed the SOL test during the 2024-2025 school year. *(Keywords: 2024-2025 numerator, 2024 passed, 2025 passed)*
* **`2024-2025 Denominator`** (*int*): The total number of students who took the SOL test during the 2024-2025 school year. *(Keywords: 2024-2025 denominator, 2024 total tested, 2025 total tested)*
* **`2024-2025 Pass Rate`** (*int*): The percentage of students who passed the SOL test in 2024-2025. *(Keywords: 2024-2025 pass rate, 2024 pass-rate, 2025 pass-rate)*
