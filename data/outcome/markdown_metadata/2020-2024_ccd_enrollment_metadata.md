# 2020-2024_ccd_enrollment.csv

**Description:** Student enrollment demographic numbers.

- **Source Organization:** Common Core of Data (CCD)
- **Geographic Coverage:** school
- **Temporal Coverage:** 2020-2024
- **URL Source:** [Link](https://educationdata.urban.org/documentation/index.html)
- **Spatial Alignment:** FIPS Column: ``, Locality Column: ``

### Column Schema

* **`year`** (*int*): The specific school or academic year that the enrollment data was collected. *(Keywords: year, school year, academic year, time period)*
* **`ncessch`** (*int*): The 12-digit unique national public school ID used to cross-reference school directory details. *(Keywords: ncessch, school id)*
* **`enrollment`** (*int*): The total integer count of students enrolled in this specific subcategory slice. *(Keywords: enrollment, student count, enrollment size, student population)*
* **`leaid`** (*int*): The 7-digit unique Local Education Agency ID mapping the school directly to its school district. *(Keywords: leaid, district, school district)*
* **`grade`** (*string*): The specific student grade level or instructional level category for the enrollment count. *(Keywords: grade, grade level)*
* **`race`** (*string*): The demographic or racial/ethnic category of the student population for the reported school enrollment count (e.g., White, Black, Hispanic, Asian, American Indian). *(Keywords: race, ethnicity, demographics)*
* **`sex`** (*string*): The demographic student gender category used to filter the student counts. *(Keywords: sex, gender)*
